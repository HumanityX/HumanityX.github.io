About Kacie

Dr. Kacie McCollum is a doctoral chair for the School of Advanced Studies and faculty member for the College of Humanities and Sciences at the University of Phoenix. She is the CEO, Shiny Bird Farms, LLC; Kacie McCollum has a B.A. in Political Science from Benedict College in Columbia, SC. She holds a M.Ed. and Ed.D. Secondary Teacher Education Program (STEP)/ Curriculum Design and Instruction from the University of Massachusetts in Amherst, MA. Dr. Kacie McCollum is committed to launching Humanity X as a new business. Dr. McCollum has 25 years of experience as a higher education administrator and business leader. She will use her knowledge, leadership experience, creativity, and expansive business network to support the growth of Humanity X. Dr. McCollum will serve as the director of operations which will provide leadership and organization to the everyday operations and administration of the business.

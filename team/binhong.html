About BinHong

Bin Hong Lee is an undergraduate student at Arizona State University in the Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering majoring in Software Engineering. He is a Gold Award winner in the 2010 World Robot Olympiad. Bin Hong will fill the gap between the technical development and the business model development. He has experience in participating in multiple hackathons, and taken entrepreneurship courses during his degree program. Bin Hong is able to understand the technical needs of the ARKHumanity technology from both ends and find the optimized point in between for the best execution. Bin Hong is committed to launching and supporting this business by consistently reevaluating and redirecting the direction of the project for social good. Bin Hong will serve as the Director of Product Development & Outreach.
